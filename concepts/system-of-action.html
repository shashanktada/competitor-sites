What is a system of action

In most supply chains, planning isn’t the bottleneck. Execution is. A system of action refers to platforms designed to coordinate decisions once disruption happens.

Why this shows up

Teams usually already have ERP, planning tools, and dashboards. The gap is coordination — who acts, how fast, and with what data.

Where it sits

Layer Role
Record Store transactions
Planning Generate forecasts and plans
Insight Provide visibility
Action Drive coordinated response

When it matters

Most relevant in high-variability environments where plans break frequently and teams rely on manual coordination.
