Alternatives to Palantir in supply chains

Palantir is typically used as a data and modeling layer. In supply chain use cases, teams often compare it against planning systems or execution-focused platforms depending on the problem.

Where Palantir fits

Strong in data integration and modeling. Less structured for planning workflows. Requires internal engineering maturity.

Blue YonderOptimization + planning
