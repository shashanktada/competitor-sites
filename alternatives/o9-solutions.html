Alternatives to o9 in supply chains

o9 is usually evaluated as a planning-led platform with strong scenario and modeling workflows. Teams typically look for alternatives when they want a different planning model, a tighter fit with existing enterprise systems, or a platform that is closer to execution.

See alternatives View comparison
Context

Where o9 usually fits

o9 is commonly used in planning-heavy environments where the operating model depends on scenario analysis, integrated business planning, and structured cross-functional planning cycles.

Representative alternatives

Platforms teams often evaluate alongside o9

Alternative 1

Kinaxis

Usually compared when concurrent planning speed and responsiveness matter more than broader scenario-modeling flexibility.

Best for concurrent planning
Alternative 2

SAP IBP

Often considered by companies that want planning capabilities tightly aligned to an existing SAP environment and operating model.

Best for SAP-centered enterprises
Alternative 3

Blue Yonder

Relevant when forecasting, optimization, and replenishment are more central than broader integrated planning orchestration.

Best for optimization-led planning
Alternative 4

Palantir

Considered when the priority shifts from packaged planning workflows to a more flexible data and modeling environment.

Best for custom data environments
Alternative 5

TADA

Relevant when the planning layer is no longer the main issue and the operational gap is coordinated response across teams.

Best for action-oriented execution
